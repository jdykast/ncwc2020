NorCal Winter Camp

January 24-26, 2020

Deeply Spiritual. Intensely Practical!

TOur Christianity is a beautiful balance of learning and living. We all need to grow in the knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. We need to hear Him and learn of Him. And then we need to live what we’ve learned. We need to walk what we’ve learned.

Paul puts it this way in Colossians 2:6 “As you therefore have received Christ Jesus the Lord, so walk in Him.”

This year we want camp to be a time of equipping our students to walk out their Christianity in practical ways. We want to help each student see what it means to receive Jesus and then walk in Him. We want each student to see that this calling is for them!

So join us for a weekend of powerful Bible teaching, passionate worship, and meaningful fellowship. Join us for a weekend set apart to God!

NorCal Winter Camp is being hosted by Crossroads Church.
